MISSION OBJECTIVES
OUTREACH

Our Outreach team is dedicated to inspiring the next generation of engineers and scientists. We engage with local schools, organize workshops, and participate in community events to share our passion for space exploration and engineering. Through these initiatives, we aim to promote STEM education and create opportunities for hands-on learning experiences.

  • Queen's Design Fairs
  • Highschools across Ontario
  • Scout and Cadet Camps
DESIGN

The Design team focuses on innovative solutions to HERC's obstacle course. This involves everything from initial concept sketches to technical documentation for both mechanical and electrical systems, ensuring pushes the boundaries of what's possible in space exploration vehicle design.

  • Cad Structural Modeling
  • PCB Design and printing
BUILD

Our Build team transforms designs into reality through careful fabrication and assembly. Using a combination of traditional and modern manufacturing techniques, we construct each component of the rover with precision and attention to detail, ensuring durability and performance in challenging conditions.

  • Aluminum Sand Casting
  • Bike Assembly
  • RC Rover Assembly
RACE

The Race team analyzes course conditions and designs the rover to maximize our success in the NASA Human Exploration Rover Challenge. Our team ensures both pilots are well-prepared for the physical and technical demands of the competition.

  • Rough Terrain
  • Obstacle Navigation
  • RC Spectrometry tasks
